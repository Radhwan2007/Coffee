Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer coffee tasting events or workshops?

Yes! We host weekly coffee tasting events every Thursday evening and monthly brewing workshops. Check our Events page for the current schedule and to reserve your spot. Private group workshops can also be arranged by contacting our events team.

Can I book your space for private events?

Absolutely. Our caf√© is available for private events after hours. We can accommodate groups of 20-50 people and offer catering options with our coffee and pastries. Please email events@beanhaven.com for availability and pricing.

Do you sell gift cards?

Yes, we offer both physical and e-gift cards that can be purchased in-store or online. They never expire and can be used for any of our products including coffee beans, drinks, and merchandise.

Where do you source your coffee beans from?

We work directly with small farms and cooperatives in Latin America, Africa, and Asia. All our coffee is ethically sourced through direct trade relationships that pay farmers 25% above fair trade prices. Visit our "About" page to learn more about our sourcing practices.

Do you offer wholesale coffee for businesses?

Yes, we provide wholesale coffee beans and training services to restaurants, offices, and other businesses. Our wholesale team can create custom blends for your establishment. Email wholesale@beanhaven.com for our wholesale menu and pricing.
