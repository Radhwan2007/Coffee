Our Coffee Story

Welcome to Bean Haven

Founded in 2010 in the heart of the city, Bean Haven began as a small dream between two college friends who shared a passion for exceptional coffee. What started as a humble corner café has blossomed into a beloved community gathering place where every cup tells a story.

We travel the world to source only the finest specialty grade coffee beans, building direct relationships with farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Our master roaster carefully crafts each batch to highlight the unique characteristics of every origin.

More than just a coffee shop, we're a place where connections are made over steaming mugs, where the aroma of freshly ground beans welcomes you home, and where every barista knows not just how to craft your drink, but knows your name too.

- The Bean Haven Family

Our Core Values

Quality Above All

We reject 98% of coffee samples we receive, accepting only the top specialty grade beans that meet our exacting standards for flavor profile and ethical production.

Farmers First

Our direct trade model ensures farmers earn 2-3 times more than conventional fair trade prices, with long-term contracts that support community development.

Sustainable Future

From compostable packaging to energy-efficient roasting, we're carbon neutral since 2018 and working toward becoming a zero-waste operation by 2025.

Education & Craft

We offer free weekly coffee classes and barista training because we believe an educated community makes for better coffee experiences for everyone.

Community Hub

Our space is designed to foster connection - with no wifi on weekends to encourage real conversations, and local art rotating monthly on our walls.

Constant Innovation

Our R&D team is always experimenting with new fermentation processes, brewing methods, and flavor combinations to push coffee forward.
